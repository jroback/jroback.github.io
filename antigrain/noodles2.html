Squash and sweet potato flour noodles (contains rice)

June 04, 2014

  • :Cook: 10 min
  • ¡2-4
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Brown Rice flour
  • 1/3 cup Anti-Grain Squash flour
  • 3 Tbsp Anti-Grain Sweet Potato flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp Xanthan gum
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Coconut oil (melted)
  • 1 tsp sea salt
Directions:

  1. Fill a medium pot with cold, salted water and bring to a boil.

  2. Place all ingredients listed in high powered blender or stand mixer and pulse until dough is formed

  3. Remove dough from blender and roll into a ball on a smooth surface dusted with the flour mixture.

  4. Use a rolling pin to gradually roll the dough into a thin layer.

  5. Slice/cut/roll into whatever shape pasta your heart desires (I used a pizza cutter to get thin noodle strips).

  6. Separate the peices and place in the boiling pot of water for 4-6 minutes.

  7. Strain and rinse with cold water.
