Sesame Seed Bagels - Sweet Potato flour and almond flour

May 05, 2014

;

Recipe

r
Cook Info:
  • :Prep: 15 min
  • :Cook: 30 min
  • ¡6-8 Bagels
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Anti-Grain Sweet Potato flour
  • 1 cup blanched almond flour
  • 2 Tbsp. Flax seed meal or chia meal
  • 1 Tbsp. Quinoa flour or tapioca flour
  • 2 Tbsp. Coconut flour
  • 2 Tbsp. Sesame seeds
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. sea salt
  • 1/4 cup raw honey or agave
Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a donut pan or cookie sheet with coconut oil.

  2. Place almond flour, sweet potato flour, coconut flour, flax meal, quinoa flour, baking soda and salt into a food processor. Process for 1 minute. Next add in the eggs, raw honey and apple cider vinegar. Process or beat for 2 minutes until mixture is completely blended.

  3. Scoop the batter into the donut slots, or form patties on the cookie sheet and use your finger to form a hole in the middle. Once formed, brush the tops of the bagels with a bit of oil and sprinkle the sesame seeds over top. Bake for 25 minutes (check at 20 minutes if you live at a low elevation). Remove the bagels and let them cool for 20 minutes.

  4. Enjoy fresh or toasted and be sure to try them with almond butter!
