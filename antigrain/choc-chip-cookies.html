Apple flour and squash flour dark chocolate chip cookies

June 11, 2014

;

Recipe

r
Cook Info:
  • :Prep: 10 min
  • :Cook: 20 min
  • ¡8-12 Cookies
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup Anti-Grain apple flour
  • 1/2 cup Anti-Grain squash flour
  • 2 Tbsp chia or flax flour
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup raw sugar
  • 3 Tbsp agave nectar
  • 1 Tbsp of almond butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 6oz dark chocolate chips
Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

  2. Combine raw sugar, agave nectar, melted coconut oil, almond butter, and egg in a large bowl.

  3. In a separate bowl, stir together apple flour, squash flour, chia flour, baking powder, ground cinnamon, and salt.

  4. Dissolve the baking soda with the almond milk in a separate bowl or cup and then stir into the wet ingredients.

  5. Slowly add the dry mixture to the wet mixture and mix well. Stir for about one minute with a spatula.

  6. Add vanilla and chocolate chips and stir until combined.

  7. Grease baking sheet or line with parchment paper. Drop cookies on by the spoonful. Bake at 325 for 12-20 minutes.
