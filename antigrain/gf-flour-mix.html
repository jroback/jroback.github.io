Ingredients:
  • 2 cups brown rice flour
  • 1 cup Anti-Grain apple flour
  • 1 cup Anti-Grain squash flour
  • 1 cup sweet rice flour
Directions:

  1. Combine all into flours into a large airtight container and combine.

  2. When ready, use the pre-mixed flour like you would all-purpose and add in any xanthan gum or chia seed/psyllium husk powder as directed.

  3. So far this mix has worked great to make bagels, noodles and pretzels.
