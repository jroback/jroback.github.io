Apple flour and almond flour savory biscuits

April 23, 2014

;

Recipe

r
Cook Info:
  • :Prep: 15 min
  • :Cook: 20 min
  • ¡8-10 Biscuits
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Anti-Grain Apple flour
  • 2 cups Almond flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp honey
Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl using a fork. Make a well in the center and add the wet ingredients. Stir wet ingredients in center, then stir in dry ingredients until a dough is formed.

  3. Remove the dough from the bowl and knead until it doesn't stick to anything other than itself. If too wet, add a bit more apple/almond flour. If too dry, add a bit more lemon juice or a touch of water.

  4. divide into 8-10 small balls and place them onto a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Flatten the dough to 1-1/2 inches using a small piece of parchment paper.

  5. Bake until lightly browned and cooked through, about 20 minutes.
