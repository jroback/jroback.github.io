"Find your inner abs" claims this manly Greek Yogurt. I've been meaning to give it a try for a while and I recently came across a BOGO coupon that pushed me over the edge.

I'm sort of in love with their tone: Axe deodorant meets Muscle Milk (see some of their viral ads here): I like the lightheartedness of their serious in a not-so-serious type of way, approach.

The package itself reminds me a lot of that of a protein powder or recovery drink. Obviously what they were going for. On inspection of the label I see that it boasts 20g of protein. Pretty nice, and definitely on par with what you see in protein bars and recovery drinks. However, weighing in at 1.5x that of a Chobani or Fage cup. (227g vs 150g) I see it also contains 1.5x the calories and 1.5x the protein. Is it just a Chobani in a 1.5x bigger cup?

Taste Test:

Tastes pretty good. It's one of those fruit on the bottom type of deals, and there is actually quite a bit of fruit in thereâ€”a manly amount of mango. Much more than in a Chobani. The yogurt itself was a slightly thicker strain than a Chobani but the texture was a bit too gritty for my taste. I'm questioning the quality just a little bit.

Overall though, not bad. But for the price, I think I'd rather just eat two Chobani's.
