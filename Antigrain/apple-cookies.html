Apple Cinnamon Cookies: Apple flour and Almond flour

Feb 28, 2014

;

Recipe

r
Cook Info:
  • :Prep: 15 min
  • :Cook: 10 min
  • ¡10-12 cookies
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup Anti-Grain apple flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/2 cup raw sugar
  • 1 Tbsp (heaping) of almond or peanut butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 6oz dark chocolate chips
Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees

  2. Combine raw sugar, melted coconut oil, almond butter, and egg in a large bowl.

  3. In a separate bowl, stir together apple flour, almond flour, baking powder, ground cinnamon, and salt.

  4. Dissolve the baking soda with the almond milk in a separate bowl or cup and stir into the wet ingredients.

  5. Slowly add dry mixture to wet mixture and mix well. Stir for about one minute with a spatula.

  6. Add vanilla and chocolate chips and stir until combined.

  7. Grease baking sheet or line with parchment paper. Drop cookies on by the spoonful. Bake at 350 for 9-12 minutes.
