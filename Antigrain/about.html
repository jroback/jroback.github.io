About Anti-Grain

The story of Anti-Grain Foods, the people behind it, and some helpful tips and tricks for using our flours in your recipes.

Vibrant alternatives for Grain-Haters*

We're not just taking the grain out of food--we won't make something unless we can replace it with something healthy, substantial, and beneficial to your body. That's a promise.

*There are a lot of reasons to hate grains: Whether you're

  • paleo
  • grain-free
  • wheat-free
  • gluten-free
  • allergen-free
  • or just trying to cut back on grains

We're not going to leave you hanging:

We experiment with our products constantly and tap the knowlege of the biggest & baddest Grain-Haters out there to keep you inspired and empowered no matter where you fall on the Grain-Hating spectrum.
