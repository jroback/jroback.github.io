Apple Flour
100% U.S.A Made

Like all our flours, Anti-Grain Apple flour contains just a single ingredient: Apples. The Apples are plucked from Northern Spy Apple trees then drum-dried and milled (skin and all) right here in the U.S. The result is a sweet, versatile, and all-natural flour that [when used regularly for biscuits, cookies, pies etc..] promises to keep the doctor away for a very, very long time.
