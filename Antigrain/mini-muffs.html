Recipe: Squash and almond flour banana muffins

Dec 20, 2013

  • :Cook: 18 min
  • ¡5
Ingredients:
  • .5 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup Anti-Grain squash flour
  • .5 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tbsp. coconut oil, softened
  • 2 Tbsp. organic raw honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 large banana, mashed
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • Optional add-ins: raisins, berries, chopped nuts, cocoa nibs, etc.
Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees

  2. Combine almond flour, squash flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl and set aside

  3. In a separate bowl, mix coconut oil, honey, almond milk, eggs, and vanilla

  4. Mix wet ingredients until fully combined and then add the mashed bananas

  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients in one bowl

  6. Pour batter into a mini-muffin tin greased with coconut oil

  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes - tops will be lightly browned and a toothpick should come out clean

  8. Let cool and then serve. Try em plain or with almond butter!
